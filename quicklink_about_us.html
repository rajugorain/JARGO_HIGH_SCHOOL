Welcome To Jargo High School

JARGO HIGH SCHOOL (H.S.) was established in 1956 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Rural area. It is located in Village- Jargo JHALDA-I block of PURULIYA district of West Bengal. The school consists of Grades from 5 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Bengali is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April. The school has Government building. It has got 26 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher. The school has Partial boundary wall. The school has have electric connection. The source of Drinking Water in the school is Well and it is functional. The school has 1 boys toilet and it is functional. and 2 girls toilet and it is functional. The school has a playground. The school has a library and has 2320 books in its library. The school does not need ramp for disabled children to access classrooms. The school has 6 computers for teaching and learning purposes and all are functional. The school is not having a computer aided learning lab. The school is Provided and Prepared in School Premises providing mid-day meal.
